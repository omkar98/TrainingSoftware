Course Overview:

Almost everyone is connected on Whatsapp, Instagram and Facebook. Ever wondered how these applications work in the backend? Wondered how Netflix provides seamless services to its users? Or how PayPal manages thousands of transactions? When you request a ride on Uber, what's the underlying technology behind it? Well, its React-JS! 

Why Learn React?

The features of ReactJS have made it immensely popular that almost all the tech giants are switching to ReactJS, and hence, looking for ReactJS Developers. In fact, the Stackoverflow's Developer's Survey states that Javascript is the Most Popular Technology in the industry today, but there are fewer developers currently to cater the need of this industry, hence paving a path for its high demand. Learning to code in React will henceforth offer you a quick hire in this demanding profile! 

Outcome:

Of course, an e-certificate. But what really you get is - to grow as a developer and build projects, apply for ReactJS internships/Freelancing, Hackathon Projects etc. The opportunities are wide open! 

Pre-requisite:Your interest and motive to learn React!
